FAQ

How often do you provide predictions?

The number of predictions depends on the chosen plan. Standard - 3-5 predictions per week, VIP - 5-7 predictions, Premium - 7-10 predictions. We don't just provide many predictions, but carefully select the most promising matches.

What's your prediction success rate?

Our average success rate over the last 12 months is 78-85% depending on the plan. Premium predictions show the highest success rate - 83-87%. However, it's important to remember that there are no 100% guarantees in betting.

What types of bets do you recommend?

We specialize in several bet types: win/draw (1X2), totals (Over/Under), Asian handicaps, both teams to score. For each match we indicate the most promising betting option in our opinion.

How does payment work?

We accept payments in cryptocurrencies: USDT (TRC20 and ERC20), Bitcoin, and through Polygon. After selecting a plan you'll be redirected to the payment page with instructions.

How quickly will I get access after payment?

Access is activated within 1-12 hours after payment confirmation on the blockchain. For USDT (TRC20) it's usually 5-15 minutes, for ERC20 - up to 1 hour, for Bitcoin - up to 12 hours depending on network load.
